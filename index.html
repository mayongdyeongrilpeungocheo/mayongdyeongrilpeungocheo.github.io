Welcome To Koshland (마용뎡릴븐곷어)

The Socialist Technocratic Benevolent Dictatorship of Koshland (마용뎡릴븐곷어) is a micronational benevolent socialist dictatorship founded in January 2024 by duropkm, also known as linuxfandudeguy.

The main currency is 홍 (Ħ).

About Koshland

Koshland is a socialist benevolent dictatorship that was founded in January 2024 after duropkm had turned his previous fictonal country, Soganland, into a real micronation. He was very inspired by the old Dutch flag, he decided to stick with orange, white and blue in all of the flag designs, then in 2025, the country shifted to socialism, benevolent authoritarianism and technocracy.
