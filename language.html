The language of Koshland

In Koshland, we speak a language called Rüdyu (르두)

The consonants are:

Manner \ Place Bilabial Alveolar Postalveolar Palatal Velar
Plosives p / b d k / g
Nasals m n ɲ ŋ
Fricatives s h
Affricates d͡ʒ / ͡ʧ
Approximants ɹ
Lateral l

And the vowels are:

Height \ Backness Front Central Back
Close i   yi u   yu   w
Close-mid e   ye   we   wi o   wo
Mid oe   j
Open-mid ae   yae eo   yeo wa   wae
Open a   ya
Rounded Front ü


writing systems

Rüdyu has 2 writing systems; one is Hangul and the other is Hanzi. (Hangul is more common.)


phonotactics

The syllable structure is CV/CVC/V/VC

The word order is SVO. (like English)

n → nh / _ # (Example: 한/漢 ‘hanh’)

ㅇ → [ŋ] / _#

ㅇ → ∅ / elsewhere (Example: 어 'eo') (Example: 앙 ‘ang’)

g → k / _ ]σ (Example: 극 ‘gük’) r → l / _ ]σ (Example: 릴 ‘ril’) d → t / _ ]σ (Example: 뎓 ‘dyeot’)


roots

ㄴ-ㅈ-ㅎ (n-j-h) - words about light (Example: 나져해 ‘light, beam, ray’ ㅅ-ㅁ (s-m) - numbers 0-5 ㄹ-ㄷ (r-d) - ordinals 1-5 ㅎ-ㄱ (h-g) - colors ㅍ-ㅅ-ㅈ (p-s-j) - temporal object There was also a root relating to plants, but it is forgotten.


Example sentence

히교홀칠보라.

"(The) purple dutch flower."
