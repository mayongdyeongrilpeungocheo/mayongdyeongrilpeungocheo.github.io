The language of Koshland

In Koshland, we speak a language called Rüdyu (르두)

The consonants are:

Manner \ Place Bilabial Alveolar Postalveolar Palatal Velar
Plosives p / b d k / g
Nasals m n ɲ ŋ
Fricatives s h
Affricates d͡ʒ / ͡ʧ
Approximants ɹ
Lateral l

And the vowels are:

Height \ Backness Front Central Back
Close i   yi u   yu   w
Close-mid e   ye   we   wi o   wo
Mid oe   j
Open-mid ae   yae eo   yeo wa   wae
Open a   ya
Rounded Front ü


writing systems

Rüdyu has 2 writing systems; one is Hangul and the other is Hanzi.


Example sentence

히교홀칠보라.

"(The) purple dutch flower."
